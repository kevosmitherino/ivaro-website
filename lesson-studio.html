Frequently asked questions

Can Ivaro create standards-aligned lesson plans?

Yes. Lesson Studio builds every lesson around the standards you're teaching, and can align vertically across grade levels so skills build on each other. You choose the grade, subject, and standards, and it drafts a complete, aligned lesson you can adjust.

How long does it take to plan a lesson?

Most lessons come together in minutes rather than hours. You can draft a single lesson or build out an entire week or unit at once, then refine anything you'd like. The goal is to keep planning inside your workday instead of your evenings and weekends.

Does Lesson Studio support differentiation and accommodations?

It does. Differentiation support and accommodation suggestions are built into the planning process, so lessons are ready for the range of learners in your room from the start, not something you have to layer on afterward.

Is Lesson Studio built for K–8 teachers specifically?

Yes. Ivaro is designed by educators for K–8 classrooms, so the pacing, language, and lesson structures fit the realities of elementary and middle school teaching rather than a generic template.
