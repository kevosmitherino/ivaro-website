Targeted support, without the guesswork or the paperwork

Every teacher knows which students are falling behind. The hard part is figuring out exactly where the gap is, what to do about it, and then documenting all of it for MTSS or RTI while still teaching everyone else.

Read more

That combination is where good intentions quietly stall. Intervention Studio helps you close that loop. It identifies skill gaps, recommends targeted interventions, and builds small groups and reteaching activities you can actually run, then helps you monitor progress and keep the documentation that MTSS and RTI require, without a separate pile of forms.

It's designed for K–8 teachers who want to give struggling students a real, specific next step, and to spend that energy on the intervention itself rather than on the tracking around it.

Frequently asked questions

How does Intervention Studio identify who needs help?

It analyzes student performance to pinpoint specific skill gaps, then recommends targeted interventions, so instead of a general sense that a student is behind, you get a clear picture of where the gap is and what to try next.

Does it support MTSS and RTI?

Yes. Intervention Studio helps you plan tiered support, monitor progress over time, and keep the documentation MTSS and RTI require, so the record-keeping happens alongside the teaching rather than as a separate task.

Can it help me plan the actual small groups?

It can. Intervention Studio builds student groupings, generates intervention lessons and reteaching activities, and lays out small-group plans you can run right away, so identifying a need turns directly into a plan of action.
