The support most teachers wish they had on tap

Teaching is one of the few jobs where you make hundreds of consequential decisions a day, often with no one to talk them through with. A great coach or mentor changes everything, but most teachers only get that in scattered moments, if at all.

Read more

The Instructional Partner is built to be there in between. Ask it how to reteach a concept that isn't landing, how to reach a student who's checked out, how to prep for a tough parent conversation, or simply how to get through a hard week. It draws on instructional best practice to give you grounded, practical guidance, and, just as importantly, it looks out for you, with encouragement and wellness support built in.

It's designed for K–8 teachers who want thoughtful help with the craft of teaching and a bit of steady support for themselves, available whenever they need it.

Frequently asked questions

What is the Ivaro Instructional Partner?

It's an always-available thought partner built into Ivaro, a place to get practical instructional coaching, classroom and student support ideas, planning help, and encouragement, without waiting for a coach's schedule or a PD day.

Is it only about instruction, or does it support me too?

Both. Alongside teaching strategies and classroom support, the Instructional Partner includes teacher wellness: stress-relief and reset activities, mindfulness and breathing exercises, burnout prevention, and encouragement, because sustainable teaching means looking after the teacher, not just the lesson.

How is this different from the other Ivaro studios?

The studios build specific things: lessons, materials, grades, projects, interventions. The Instructional Partner is the conversation around all of it, the coach you turn to for advice, decisions, and a bit of steady support as you use the rest of Ivaro.
