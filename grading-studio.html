Faster grading that still sees the student

Grading is where a teacher's week goes to disappear. The stack never really shrinks, feedback gets shorter the later it gets, and by the time work comes back, the moment to act on it has often passed.

Speeding that up shouldn't mean caring less about what the work actually shows. Grading Studio lets you scan or upload student work and get consistent, standards-based scoring alongside feedback that's actually useful, to the student and to you. Frequently asked questions

Is student data kept private?

Yes. Grading Studio is intentionally designed with FERPA and COPPA compliance in mind. Student work and data are never sold and never used for advertising. Privacy is a starting requirement, not an add-on.

How does Grading Studio speed up grading?

You scan or upload student work and get consistent, standards-based scoring plus draft feedback in a fraction of the time. It handles the repetitive part so you can focus your attention where your judgment actually matters.

Can it help me see how my class is doing overall?

Yes. Beyond individual scores, Grading Studio surfaces class mastery, trends, missing assignments, and student growth, and can produce parent-friendly progress summaries, so you can act on patterns, not just grades.
