The materials your lesson needs, without the busywork

Every good lesson comes with a long tail of small documents — an exit ticket to check for understanding, a quick quiz, a practice worksheet, a set of discussion questions, a study guide before the test. Individually they're minor. Together, they quietly eat an entire prep period, and then some.

Deliverable Studio creates those materials for you. Tell it the standard, skill, or lesson you're working on, and it drafts classroom-ready student documents you can use as-is or tweak to fit your class — matched to your grade level and ready to print, assign, or drop into your LMS.

It's built for K–8 teachers who want fewer late nights making worksheets and more time actually teaching — with materials that stay aligned to what you're teaching, not pulled from a random search.

Frequently asked questions

What kinds of materials can Deliverable Studio create?

A wide range — exit tickets, quizzes, worksheets, homework, guided and independent practice, graphic organizers, vocabulary activities, writing prompts, choice boards, warm-ups, review games, and study guides, among others. If it's a document you'd normally make by hand for your lesson, Deliverable Studio is built to draft it for you.

Are the materials aligned to what I'm teaching?

Yes. You tell Deliverable Studio the standard, skill, or lesson, and it generates materials that match — so your exit ticket, worksheet, and quiz all reinforce the same objective instead of pulling from unrelated sources.

Can I edit what it generates?

Always. Everything is a starting draft you stay in control of. Adjust the wording, difficulty, or format to fit your class, then print it, assign it, or drop it into your LMS.
